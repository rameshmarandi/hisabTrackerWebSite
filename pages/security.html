Data Security

Last updated: January 2026

1. Your Data Ownership

You fully own your financial data. Hisab Tracker does not claim any ownership over your transactions, records, or reports.

2. Local Device Storage

By default, all your data is stored securely on your device. We do not upload your information to our servers unless you explicitly enable cloud backup.

3. Cloud Sync Protection

If cloud sync is enabled, your data is encrypted during transfer and storage. We use industry standard security practices to prevent unauthorized access.

4. No Selling of Data

We never sell, rent, or share your personal or financial information with advertisers, marketers, or third parties.

5. Access Control

Only you can access your data within the app. We do not have access to view, modify, or analyze your personal financial records.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time directly from the app. If cloud sync is enabled, synced data will also be permanently removed from our servers.

7. Security Updates

We regularly update the app to fix vulnerabilities and improve protection against new threats.

8. Contact

For any data security related questions, contact us at helphisabtracker@gmail.com
