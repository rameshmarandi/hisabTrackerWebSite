Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Hisab Tracker (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use the Hisab Tracker mobile application, website, and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate and improve the app:

  • Contact information such as name and email when you contact support or submit feedback.
  • Device information including model, OS version, and crash logs for stability and security.
  • Usage data to understand app performance and improve features.
  • Optional cloud backup data if you explicitly enable synchronization.

2. Financial & Transaction Data

All your financial records, transactions, balances, and notes are stored locally on your device by default. We do not access, read, analyze, sell, or share your personal financial data. If you enable cloud backup, your data is encrypted and stored securely only for restoration purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain app functionality.
  • To improve performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • To respond to support queries and technical issues.
  • To notify you about important updates, security alerts, and policy changes.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encryption, secure servers, access control, and regular security reviews. Your data is protected against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, and destruction.

5. Cloud Backup & Sync

Cloud backup is optional. When enabled, your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Only you can restore your data using your authenticated account. We do not read or use your backup data.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for:

  • Crash reporting and performance monitoring.
  • Cloud storage and infrastructure.
  • Analytics to improve app quality.

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and comply with applicable privacy laws.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or financial data to anyone. Information is shared only if legally required by government authorities or to comply with law enforcement.

8. User Rights & Control

You have full control over your data. You can:

  • Export your data at any time.
  • Delete your cloud backup.
  • Uninstall the app to remove local data.
  • Request account deletion where applicable.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. Local data is stored only on your device unless cloud backup is enabled.

10. Children’s Privacy

Hisab Tracker is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. International Users

Your information may be processed on servers located in different countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

12. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of the app implies acceptance of the updated policy.
