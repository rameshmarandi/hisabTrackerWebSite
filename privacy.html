Privacy Policy

Learn how we protect your financial data with military-grade security and respect your privacy.

Last Updated: December 1, 2023

Introduction

At Hisab Tracker, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services. Please read this privacy policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number (optional)
  • Financial Data: Expense entries, income records, transaction details, category information
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Feature usage patterns, app preferences, settings
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining our services
  • Processing your financial data to generate insights
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Sending important updates and notifications
  • Customer support and troubleshooting
  • Security monitoring and fraud prevention
  • Legal compliance and regulatory requirements

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • 256-bit Encryption: All financial data encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Local Storage: Your primary data stays on your device
  • Secure Cloud: Optional cloud backup with end-to-end encryption
  • Access Controls: Multi-factor authentication for premium users
  • Regular Audits: Security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Employee Training: Strict confidentiality agreements with staff

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal or financial data. We may share information only in these cases:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With service providers under strict confidentiality agreements
  • In aggregated, anonymized form for analytics

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in standard formats
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent at any time

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Improve user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Services

We use these third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics (website analytics)
  • Firebase (crash reporting and analytics)
  • Payment processors (for premium subscriptions)
  • Cloud storage providers (for encrypted backups)

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • As long as your account is active
  • For legal compliance requirements
  • Until you request deletion
  • Backup data for 30 days after account deletion

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than India. We ensure adequate protection through standard contractual clauses and encryption.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email. Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.
